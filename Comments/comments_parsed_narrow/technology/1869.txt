I've found it useful using LLMs to help understand company 10-K reports like these:

I'm certain financial professionals are using it to better understand the world. The article says it uses ChatGPT for sentiment analysis on headlines, but I think an even bigger leap is going to be getting actual meaning out of news and financial reports more efficiently.
