There is one part of software engineering and development that ChatGPT will never be able to really fill in though. Legacy systems, sure you can get ChatGPT to write a COBOL script, and when there’s very little supply and a bit more demand for them, I’m sure that it’ll come in handy. But you tell anyone who isn’t tech savvy that the code was partially generated by an AI and you’re gonna get a lot of glances. People like being able to see who develops the code, even if it is just some guy who talks in like 2 meetings a week
