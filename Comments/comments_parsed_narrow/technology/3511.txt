Teachers aren’t paid enough to come up with teaching methods that aren’t the usual routine homework/assignment/memorization methods.  If the only focus is on the final product, the students will keep getting that final product completed by an AI assistant.  It’s the path of least resistance and better results more often than not, and at their age they’d rather get their work done ASAP to do other things.  Schools will need to fundamentally alter their approach to offset ChatGPT’s impact.
