Look, this shouldn't be surprising.
Ideally everything would be equal but it isn't.  ChatGPT is going to take context into account to decide its output.  In fact it relies almost entirely on context.

If you have a group of people from various cultures who can all reasonably tolerate a joke on them except 1 and that person is of the mind to commit atrocious acts in response, the bot is not going to "crack a joke" at them just because of some impossible fantasy that everyone responds peacefully to the same things.
