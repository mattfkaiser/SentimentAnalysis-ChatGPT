Not completely. Detecting is probably not the same as making.
You can laugh and think someone is making a funny joke, but that isn't the same as being able to make a funny joke.
You can see someone make some impressive prompt for chatGPT, but just because you know it's impressive doesn't mean you can make impressive prompts yourself (you can try a lot of stuff until you recognize it's funny or impressive though, but that might take very long and requires you to be able to think of a lot of different possibilities)
