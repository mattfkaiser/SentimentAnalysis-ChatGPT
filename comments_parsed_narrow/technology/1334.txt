It's a *necessary* legal burden. If ChatGPT is essentially monetizing other people's IP to create its own product, they owe payment for commercial use. If ChatGPT is using content in a way that makes it impossible to do so legally, they can't operate the way they have been.

AI generation does not fit within most legal definitions of transformative work, it's closer to sampling at a massive scale that is baked into the final product and therefore everything that final product generates.
